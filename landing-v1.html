Household planning

One table.
Every person
accounted for.

Savor is the only meal planner built for how Indian households actually eat — the same meal, different portions, different restrictions, one cook, and one day a week when she's off. Everyone's profile lives in one household. The plan works for all of them.

🍽️
Ingredient reuse by design
Monday's chicken becomes Wednesday's korma. Tuesday's quinoa carries to Sunday's no-cook bowl. The week is architectured, not just planned.

No cook on Sundays? No problem. The week's prep leaves exactly what you need to assemble a full day of meals in under 30 minutes.
