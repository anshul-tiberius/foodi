Savor learns your kitchen
in four minutes.

Savor doesn't guess. It walks you through your kitchen, category by category, with a simple visual scan. Tap what you have. Skip what you don't. That's your baseline.

1
4-minute guided scan at setup
Grains, proteins, produce, spices — tap what you have. No typing. Savor has your baseline before you've finished your first cup of tea.

After each meal a single tap updates your pantry. Expiring soon? Savor plans around it. Missing something? It goes straight to your shopping list.
